NextStep: AI Career Matching for Consultants

NextStep is an AI-powered career platform that connects consultants and ex-consultants with top business roles, and helps companies hire vetted consulting talent instantly.

What NextStep Does

For Companies Hiring

NextStep delivers a curated shortlist of vetted, best-match consultants without sifting through hundreds of poor-fit applications. The platform automates the entire hiring flow: posting jobs, AI screening, and interview management.

  • Instant shortlists of qualified candidates
  • AI screening that understands business context, not just keywords
  • Vetted talent pool of consultants actively seeking roles
  • Interview management and tracking in one place

For Candidates (Consultants and Ex-Consultants)

NextStep helps consultants break out of consulting and into top-tier business roles. The platform matches candidates with curated opportunities based on experience, skills, and career goals.

  • Curated roles delivered to your inbox weekly
  • AI matching based on your background and preferences
  • Access to startups, Fortune 500s, VC-backed companies, finance, and PE
  • Companies approach you directly for interviews

Target Roles

  • Head of Operations

  • Transformation Manager

How NextStep Is Different

Quality Over Quantity

Companies receive a small, focused shortlist instead of hundreds of applications. Candidates see curated matches instead of endless job feeds. Tighter shortlists mean fewer interviews and faster decisions.

AI That Understands Context

NextStep's AI uses semantic understanding to analyze responsibilities, business context, and competencies. It matches based on actual experience and outcomes, not keyword matching.

Vetted, Ready-to-Interview Talent

Every candidate profile is vetted for relevance and current availability. Companies match against talent actively seeking roles.

All-in-One Platform

Screening, messaging, and interview review happen directly on NextStep. No extra tools or back-and-forth emails required.

Pricing

Free to get started for both companies and candidates. Companies can post jobs and see top talent without upfront cost. Candidates create profiles and receive matches at no charge. Setup takes approximately 60 seconds.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NextStep?

NextStep is an AI-powered career platform with two sides: (1) For companies: delivers curated shortlists of vetted, best-match consultants without sifting through hundreds of applications. (2) For candidates: connects consultants and ex-consultants with top-tier business roles matched to their experience and goals.

Who is NextStep for?

For companies: SMEs looking to scale and Fortune 500 business units needing proven business expertise. For candidates: consultants and ex-consultants from firms like McKinsey, BCG, and Bain looking to exit consulting into business roles.

What types of roles does NextStep match?

Business roles requiring strategic expertise: Strategy Manager/Director, Head of Operations, PMO Lead, Transformation Manager, RevOps Manager, COO, Chief of Staff, and Product Manager. Industries include startups, VC-backed companies, Fortune 500s, finance, and private equity.

How does NextStep's AI matching work?

The AI uses semantic understanding to analyze job responsibilities, business context, and required competencies. It matches candidates based on their experience, projects, outcomes, and stakeholder experience—not just keywords.

Is NextStep free to use?

Yes. Candidates can create a profile and receive job matches for free. Companies can sign up and see top talent for free. Setup takes about 60 seconds.

How fast can companies see candidates?

Instantly, if qualified matches exist. Most teams move from job description to first interviews within days. For best results, keep roles open 7-14 days to capture new matches.

Does NextStep integrate with my ATS?

No integrations required. Email candidates directly from NextStep and keep your pipeline moving. Most teams add final details to their ATS after choosing a hire.

How is NextStep different from job boards?

NextStep delivers quality over quantity. Companies get vetted shortlists, not hundreds of applications. Candidates get curated roles, not endless feeds. AI screening, messaging, and interviews are managed in one platform.
